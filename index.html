My Purpose

All that aside, I am a family man now and I am also running for Constable right here in Fayette County District 1. I will no longer be leading any more coup d'etats.

My Expertise

My main plan of action for bringing peace to our community involves the use of Geass, also known as the King's Power. With this power, I will be able to persuade any criminal to walk the straight and narrow, no matter how hardened he is in his life of crime. It will only work once, so if they are somehow able to break free then do not fear, I have Plan B!
